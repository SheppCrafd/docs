Zmanim Today is a Progressive Web App (PWA) — no app store, no download, no account. You install it directly from your browser in about 15 seconds. Once installed, it launches full-screen from your home screen or app drawer, loads faster, works offline, and can send push notification reminders for upcoming zmanim.

<Note>
  Installing is **strongly recommended** for regular use. iOS push notifications for zman reminders **only work when the app is installed** — they will not fire from a regular Safari tab.
</Note>

## Pick Your Platform

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iPhone / iPad">
    <Warning>
      iOS **requires Safari** to install PWAs. Chrome and Firefox on iOS cannot install the app.
    </Warning>

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open in Safari">
        Visit [https://zmanim-today-1f2656a3.base44.app](https://zmanim-today-1f2656a3.base44.app) in Safari.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap the Share button">
        Tap the **Share** icon (square with an upward arrow) in the bottom toolbar.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap Add to Home Screen">
        Scroll down the share sheet and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm the name">
        Keep the default name **Zmanim Today** (or edit it) and tap **Add**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Launch from your home screen">
        The Zmanim Today icon now sits on your home screen. Tap it — the app opens full-screen with no browser chrome.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android">
    Works in **Chrome**, **Edge**, **Brave**, and most Chromium-based browsers.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open in Chrome">
        Visit [https://zmanim-today-1f2656a3.base44.app](https://zmanim-today-1f2656a3.base44.app) in Chrome.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open the browser menu">
        Tap the **three-dot menu** (⋮) in the top-right corner.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap Install app">
        Tap **Install app** — or **Add to Home screen** on older versions.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm">
        Tap **Install** in the prompt.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Launch from your home screen">
        The icon appears on your home screen and in your app drawer. Tap it to open full-screen.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      Chrome often shows an **“Add Zmanim Today to Home screen”** banner at the bottom of the screen on your first visit. Tap **Install** on that banner to skip the menu steps.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Desktop (Chrome / Edge)">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open in Chrome or Edge">
        Visit [https://zmanim-today-1f2656a3.base44.app](https://zmanim-today-1f2656a3.base44.app).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click the install icon">
        Look for the **install icon** (a small computer with a down arrow) at the right end of the address bar and click it.

        If you don’t see it, open the **three-dot menu** → **Cast, save and share** → **Install Zmanim Today…**
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click Install">
        Confirm in the prompt.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Launch as a standalone app">
        Zmanim Today now opens in its own window, appears in your app launcher (Start menu, Dock, or Launchpad), and can be pinned like any native app.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="macOS Safari">
    Requires macOS Sonoma (14) or later.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open in Safari">
        Visit [https://zmanim-today-1f2656a3.base44.app](https://zmanim-today-1f2656a3.base44.app).
      </Step>
      <Step title="File → Add to Dock">
        In the Safari menu bar, click **File** → **Add to Dock…**
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm">
        Keep the default name and click **Add**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Launch from the Dock">
        The Zmanim Today icon lands in your Dock. Click to open it in its own window.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## After You Install

<Steps>
  <Step title="Grant location permission">
    On first launch, tap **Use My Location** and allow the location prompt so the app can calculate your zmanim.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable notifications (optional)">
    Tap the **bell icon** on the Home screen to open the Reminders panel and allow notifications. This is required for zman reminders on iOS.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customise your dashboard">
    Tap the **gear icon** to reorder or hide zmanim, switch to 24-hour time, or toggle dark mode.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Uninstalling

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="iOS">
    Long-press the Zmanim Today icon on your home screen, then tap **Remove App** → **Delete App**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Android">
    Long-press the icon and drag it to **Uninstall**, or open **Settings** → **Apps** → **Zmanim Today** → **Uninstall**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Desktop">
    Open the app, click the **three-dot menu** in its title bar, and choose **Uninstall Zmanim Today**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Uninstalling and reinstalling is the fastest fix if notifications ever stop working — it forces the browser to refresh permissions and re-register the service worker.
</Tip>
