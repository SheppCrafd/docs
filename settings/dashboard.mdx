The Zmanim Today home dashboard is entirely yours to configure. Choose which zmanim appear, add or remove the special widgets, and arrange everything in the exact order that suits your daily davening routine. All changes take effect instantly and are remembered across browser sessions.

## Opening the Dashboard Customiser

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    From the Home screen, tap the **Settings** gear icon (⚙) in the top-right corner of the header. Settings is not in the hamburger navigation drawer — the gear button on Home is the only entry point.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll to Home Dashboard">
    Scroll down past the Location, Appearance, and Time Format sections to reach the **Home Dashboard** section. A short line of instructional text reads _"Toggle items and drag to reorder."_
  </Step>
</Steps>

The dashboard list shows every available item — widgets first, then all zmanim — each with a drag handle on the left, an emoji icon, a label, and a checkbox toggle on the right.

## Showing and Hiding Zmanim

Each row in the dashboard list has a **checkbox** on its right edge.

- A **filled blue checkbox** means the item is currently visible on the home dashboard.
- An **empty checkbox** means the item is hidden.

Tap any checkbox to toggle its visibility. The change is saved instantly to your browser's local storage and persists for all future sessions.

## Reordering Zmanim

Every row has a **grip handle** (⠿) on its left edge. To rearrange the dashboard:

- **On mobile** — press and hold the grip handle until the row lifts slightly, then drag it up or down to the desired position and release.
- **On desktop** — click and hold the grip handle, drag to the new position, and release the mouse button.

A subtle blue highlight and drop-shadow appear on the row you are dragging to confirm it is in motion. The new order is saved automatically when you drop the item.

## Available Dashboard Items

The dashboard list contains **19 items** in total: 2 special widgets and 17 zmanim.

### Special Widgets

Both widgets are enabled by default.

| Item | Description |
| --- | --- |
| ⏱ **Next Zman Countdown** | A live countdown card that ticks down in real time to the next upcoming zman. Turns amber when fewer than 30 minutes remain. |
| 🧭 **Jerusalem Compass** | A mini compass widget showing the bearing and direction to Jerusalem from your current location. Tapping it opens the full-screen Compass page. |

<Tip>
  For a clean, focused dashboard showing only the most commonly needed times, enable just **Sunrise**, **Sof Zman Shema (GRA)**, **Mincha Gedola**, **Candle Lighting**, and **Sunset** — and place the **Next Zman Countdown** card at the top. Everything else can stay hidden until you need it.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Dashboard preferences are saved locally in your browser and persist automatically across sessions. When Zmanim Today receives an update, your saved order and toggle states carry forward without any action needed on your part. New zmanim added in future releases will appear at the bottom of the list in their default (off) state.
</Note>
