In just a few taps you can open Zmanim Today in your browser, grant location access, and immediately see a personalised dashboard showing every halachic prayer time for today at your exact location. There is no app store to visit, and no setup beyond allowing the browser's standard location prompt.

## Steps to Get Up and Running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app in your browser">
    Navigate to the [Zmanim Today](https://zmanim-today-1f2656a3.base44.app/) in any modern browser. The app works on **iOS Safari**, **Android Chrome**, **Firefox**, and all major desktop browsers. No special plugins or extensions are required — the app loads entirely in the browser tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in to your account">
    Zmanim Today requires a free account so your preferences, saved locations, and reminders sync across your devices. Sign in with **email and password**, **Google**, **Microsoft**, or **Apple**. Creating an account takes  seconds and is entirely free.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your location">
    To load zmanim, tap **Use My Location** — this triggers the browser's location permission prompt. Tap or click **Allow** to let Zmanim Today read your GPS coordinates.

    Your location is used solely to calculate accurate halachic times for your position — it is passed directly to the Hebcal API over a secure HTTPS connection and is never stored on any Zmanim Today server. If you prefer not to share your GPS position, tap the search icon and type a city name instead ([search by city](#searching-by-city)).
  </Step>
  <Step title="View your zmanim dashboard">
    Once your location is confirmed, your personalized dashboard appears instantly. You will see:

    - **Next Zman card** — a live countdown timer showing how long until the next upcoming prayer time, highlighted in amber when under 30 minutes.
    - **Zmanim list** — a scrollable summary of every enabled halachic time for today, based on your local or selected timezone.
    - **Mini Compass widget** — a small compass pointing toward Jerusalem, showing the bearing in degrees and your distance to the city.

    Tap the **Settings** gear icon in the top-right corner of the Home screen to reorder items, hide zmanim you don't need, or switch to 24-hour time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="(Optional) Install to your home screen">
    For the best experience, install Zmanim Today as an app on your device. See [Installation](/installation) for step-by-step platform instructions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Installing the app is strongly recommended for regular use and is **required** for push notification reminders on iOS. Full walk-through: [Installation](/installation).
</Tip>

## Searching by City

If you decline the browser location prompt — or if you are planning ahead for travel — you can set your location by typing a city name instead of using GPS.

On the Home screen, tap the **Search** icon (magnifying glass) next to the **Use My Location** button. A search field slides in. Type any city or place name — for example `Jerusalem`, `New York, NY`, or `London` — and tap the search button. Zmanim Today resolves the place name to coordinates via the Open-Meteo geocoding API and calculates your prayer times for that location.

Your searched location is saved locally on your device. You can clear it from the **Settings** screen (tap the gear icon on the Home header) or change it directly by tapping **Change** next to the location display on the Home screen.
