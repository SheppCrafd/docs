## Start in Four Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Zmanim Today">
    Visit the app in any modern browser. No account or sign-up is required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Allow location access">
    Tap **Allow** when the browser asks for location access. Your GPS coordinates are used only to calculate zmanim — they are never stored on a server.
  </Step>
  <Step title="See today's prayer times">
    Your personalised dashboard loads instantly with today's zmanim, a live countdown to the next prayer time, and quick links to the compass and siddur.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install as a PWA (optional)">
    Add Zmanim Today to your home screen for instant, full-screen access — no app store required. See the tip below for instructions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Install Zmanim Today as a Progressive Web App (PWA) for the best experience. On iOS, tap the **Share** button and choose **Add to Home Screen**. On Android, tap the browser menu and select **Install App**.
</Tip>
