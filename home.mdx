## Get up and running in seconds

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Zmanim Today">
    Visit the app in any modern browser — no account, no sign-up, nothing to download.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Allow location access">
    Tap **Allow** so the app can calculate zmanim for your exact position. Your coordinates go straight to the Hebcal API over HTTPS — never stored on a Zmanim Today server.
  </Step>
  <Step title="See today's prayer times">
    Your dashboard loads with a live countdown to the next zman, all of today's halachic times, and the mini Jerusalem compass.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install to your home screen">
    Pin the app for one-tap access and full-screen mode. Required for push notification reminders on iOS. See the [Installation guide](/installation).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Everything is calculated in the browser using public APIs (Hebcal, Sefaria, Open-Meteo). Zmanim Today has no server of its own and stores nothing about you — all preferences live in your browser's `localStorage`.
</Tip>
