## Get up and running in seconds

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Zmanim Today">
    Visit the app in any modern browser and sign in with your email, Google, Microsoft, or Apple account. Creating an account is free and takes seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Allow location access">
    Tap **Allow** so the app can calculate zmanim for your exact position. Your coordinates go straight to the Hebcal API over HTTPS — never stored on a Zmanim Today server.
  </Step>
  <Step title="See today's prayer times">
    Your dashboard loads with a live countdown to the next zman, all of today's halachic times, and a mini Jerusalem compass.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install to your home screen">
    Pin the app for one-tap access and full-screen mode. Required for reminders on iOS. See the [Installation guide](/installation).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Zmanim, compass math, and siddur text all run in your browser using public APIs (Hebcal, Sefaria, Open-Meteo). Preferences live in `localStorage`. The only server component is push notifications — if you enable reminders, your Web Push subscription (endpoint \+ location \+ toggles) is stored on the [base44](https://base44.com) backend so notifications can be delivered even when the app is closed.
</Tip>
